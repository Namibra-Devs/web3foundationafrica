About Us

Web3 Foundation Africa is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Web3 technology in Africa. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to embrace the future of technology, leveraging its transformative potential. We strive to create a positive, inclusive, and sustainable Web3 community in Africa.
